<h2>The Story</h2>

<p>
I design environments where brilliant minds do their best work, then I measure what happens.
</p>

<p>
I founded <a href="https://reppedlabs.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>Repped Labs</strong></a> with my daughter as a living lab where neurodistinct adults grow technical, executive functioning, and advocacy skills while the environment itself generates the research. Our data backs a simple finding: when you design for neurodistinct people, all minds benefit.
</p>

<p>
Award-winning software engineer. Doctoral-trained researcher. Educator who has taught and mentored hundreds of technologists. Everything on this page ships.
</p>
